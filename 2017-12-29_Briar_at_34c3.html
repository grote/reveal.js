* How does Briar work? * And how does this address these threats?
* Authenticated Stream Cipher with 256 bit keys * Forward Secrecy (periodic key rotation due to delay tolerance, ratcheting planned) * [Bramble Transport Protocol](https://code.briarproject.org/akwizgran/briar-spec/blob/master/protocols/BTP.md) (BTP) * How to exchange shared secret?
* meeting in person and scanning QR codes as initial key exchange * prevents MITM attacks (also a threat) * trusted peer can introduce two contacts * future: Scanning codes or entering tokens remotely * [Bramble QR Code Protocol](https://code.briarproject.org/akwizgran/briar-spec/blob/master/protocols/BQP.md) (BQP)

Cryptographic Algorithms

Authenticated Encryption:

XSalsa20/Poly1305

Hash and MAC:

BLAKE2s → BLAKE2b

Signatures:

ECDSA w/ brainpoolP256r1 → Ed25519

Key Agreement:

ECDHC w/ brainpoolP256r1 → Curve25519

* connection between peers consists of 6 Tor relays (3 picked by each peer) * hides metadata even from network observers (maybe not NSA) * still room for improvements (e.g. timing attacks)
