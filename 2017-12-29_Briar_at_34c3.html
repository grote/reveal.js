Sneakernet * only partly a joke, it really works!
* Authenticated Stream Cipher with 256 bit keys * Forward Secrecy (periodic key rotation due to delay tolerance, ratcheting planned) * How to exchange shared secret?
* meeting in person and scanning QR codes as initial key exchange * prevents MITM attacks (also a threat) * Also: Trusted peer can introduce two contacts * Future: Scanning codes or entering tokens remotely

Cryptographic Algorithms

Authenticated Encryption:

XSalsa20/Poly1305

Hash and MAC:

BLAKE2s → BLAKE2b

Signatures:

ECDSA w/ brainpoolP256r1 → Ed25519

Key Agreement:

ECDHC w/ brainpoolP256r1 → Curve25519
